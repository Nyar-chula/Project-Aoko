Knowledge and technology transfer

AMYRES is dedicated to ensuring that research findings/technologies are disseminated to farmers and other stakeholders.To enhance these efforts, AMYRES integrates digital solutions to increase productive efficiency and solve challenges within the agriculture sector.

Given the challenges in agricultural data, AMYRES offers data and information management solutions such as farm management information systems, data analytics platforms, farm advisory Apps, Digital market places, Agri-finance technologies etc.

In precision agriculture, the organization leverages technologies such as remote sensing, drone and satellite imagery, auto-guided equipment, variable rate technology to optimize productivty through data driven deceision making at the farm.

These solutions have the potential to increase productivity, reduce resource waste, improve market access, and support sustainability. They empower farmers with real-time data and actionable insights, leading to better decision-making and improved profitability across the agricultural sector.
